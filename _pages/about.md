A postdoctoral researcher with extensive experience in developing end-to-end optical sensing systems, from theoretical modeling to functional instrumentation. My work integrates plasmonics, microfluidics, and photodetector/ CMOS-based detection to create deployable sensing platforms, supported by expertise in nanofabrication, multiphysics simulation, and system-level engineering. These contributions support the advancement of practical, high-sensitivity detection technologies with real-world applications.
